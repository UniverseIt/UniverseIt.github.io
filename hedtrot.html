ABOUT HEDTROT

Hedtrot is a São Paulo-based tech startup, whose purpose is to create Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality apps and solutions. We are developing Perspective, a AR and VR gaming platform.


VIRTUAL REALITY

Virtual Reality is a way to merge into virtual ambients as if they were real. You can use head mounted displays to see this world, stereo 3D headphones to feel like you are into it and controllers and gesture recognition to interact.


AUGMENTED REALITY

Augmented Reality is just like Virtual Reality, but with a differential: you can still interact with the real world, but in a extended way - by generating virtual images that superimposes real ones. So, with Augmented Reality, you see objects that aren't physically real.


WHEN YOU MIX THESE TWO

Then you can have total immersion with virtual environments and you can still interact with your real world. This way, you can, for example, interact with a 3D city model as if it was in your table or play with dragons as if they were in your hands.

Perspective

Perspective is our killer app: have you ever dreamt of having an AR gaming platform? And a cheap one. This is what Perspective is: an AR gaming platform that uses only Google Cardboard. Take your Android smartphone, download the app, put it on your Cardboard and voila! A whole augmented reality platform. Perspective has some games: Lab, where you can manipulate 3D objects, Chess, a chess game you've never seen before and checkers.
